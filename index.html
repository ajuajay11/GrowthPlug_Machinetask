Meet the Team

Meet Dr. Vani Patibandla

Dr. Vani M. Patibandla has been practicing in the Canton, Ohio, area for 25 years. She was an undergraduate at Kenyon College, where she graduated with a degree in Biochemistry. She later went on to graduate from Case Western Reserve University School of Dentistry in 1998. She also completed a year-long general practice residency and continues her education every quarter with the local dental society and study club

Dr. Patibandla is a member of the American Dental Association®, Academy of General Dentistry, and Stark Dental Society. She is also an Ohio Dental Associate.

Her goal as a dentist is to improve her patients’ oral health while also leaving them with positive impressions by creating genuine and long-lasting relationships with them. Seeing improvement in a patient’s oral health genuinely makes her day. And creating an environment of positivity and laughter with her team results in a wonderful atmosphere for our patients to enjoy. This invites new patients to visit, many of whom have heard about us by word of mouth.

Dr. Patibandla has a wonderful husband and two beautiful daughters. When not in the office, she loves reading, going on walks and hikes, meditating, and cooking.
